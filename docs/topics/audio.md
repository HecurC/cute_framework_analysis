Cute Framework supports both music and sound FX. Music is great for switching/fading from one track to another, while sound FX are good for playing individual clips of audio. All audio files are expected to be in either .wav format (16-bit pcm) or .ogg format. It's recommended you save your audio files in 44100hz.
